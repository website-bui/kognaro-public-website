Governed workflows

Engine shipped. First live pilots Q4 2026.

A workflow is the governed unit of work in Kognaro. It holds lifecycle state, binds to a versioned definition of your business meaning, and authorizes what agents are allowed to do before any automation runs. Agents propose. The workflow decides. Automation executes only what was authorized. The engine is built. The next milestone is running it against real business processes under live conditions.
