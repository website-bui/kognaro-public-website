Kognaro

Your AI. Your Data. Your Terms.

Kognaro is a platform for governed AI agent orchestration — organizations of AI agents that work the way employees work: they propose, a workflow authorizes, and automation executes, so nothing acts on the business without recorded authority. Your data never leaves your systems of record — agents are users of those systems, not custodians of their contents, and the customer owns the data, the meaning, and the models it runs on. Trust here is not a promise about a model's intelligence; it is structural — produced by explicit meaning, clear ownership, and auditable execution, so any decision an agent took part in can be reconstructed long after the conversation that produced it is gone.
